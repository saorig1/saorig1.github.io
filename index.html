About me

I’m based in Brooklyn, NY, originally from Tokyo. At Symantec (Norton), I first started as a QA, transitioned into project management, collaborated with engineers, and trained internal teams before product launches. This work sparked my interest in how people engage with software across different technical and cultural backgrounds, which led me to pursue graduate studies in Instructional Design.

I partner closely with Customer Success to map the customer journey, build onboarding strategies, and develop learning resources. I define metrics such as platform engagement, training consumption, pass/fail rates, support case reduction, and customer feedback to measure and improve program effectiveness. I work cross-functionally with global teams and SMEs to ensure alignment on goals and deliverables. I continuously iterate on programs based on data and feedback to maximize learning outcomes.

At the heart of my work is a passion for helping individuals and businesses reach their goals through clear, relevant, and effective training. I thrive on creating solutions that empower users and drive measurable impact across teams.
